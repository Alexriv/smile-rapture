Welcome to RAPTURE

Our Mission

Our mission at RAPTURE is to revolutionize unmanned aerial systems (UAS) research and education by providing a remotely accessible platform that bridges the gap between simulated and real-world testing. We empower researchers, educators, and developers with cutting-edge tools and infrastructure, ensuring seamless experimentation and collaboration across diverse UAS applications.

What is RAPTURE?

RAPTURE stands for Remotely Accessible Platform of Testbeds for UAS Research and Education. This platform is designed to provide users with the ability to remotely access and deploy their UAS-related experiments on a variety of testbeds, facilitating innovation and education in the field of unmanned aerial systems.

About RAPTURE

RAPTURE is dedicated to advancing UAS research and education through a comprehensive, remotely accessible platform. We provide the tools and infrastructure necessary for seamless real-world testing, enabling researchers and educators to focus on innovation and discovery.
